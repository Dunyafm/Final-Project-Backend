Maison Margiela Beauty

Take your perfume collection to the next level with heavenly scents from our range of Martin Margiela fragrances, available here at Selfridges. With the philosophy of bringing your memories to life, the esteemed French designer has lovingly crafted fragrances that evoke the moments that matter to you. Whether you're craving to relive long walks on the beach or simply want to revel in the familiar cosiness of a lazy Sunday morning, be transported back with a simple spritz of an eau de toilette. 

Bring alluring notes into your home with finely executed Maison Margiela fragrances or adorn yourself with sultry scents you can take with you wherever you go. From nights curled up next to a crackling open fireplace to sun-drenched afternoons spent under lemon trees in Italy, close your eyes and allow yourself to be completely enveloped by a choice of evocative smells. 

Looking for a personal gift to treat a loved one? Try a Maison Martin Margiela cologne; they come in an array of seductive scents to suit whomever you're gifting. Or if you want to invest in a luxury fragrance that you can come back to time and time again, a Maison Martin Margiela perfume is forever in style, thanks to expertly chosen aroma combinations.
